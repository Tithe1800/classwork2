About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering has been successfully functioning since 2002. The department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) since its inception has been continuously fostering academic excellence through industry academy collaboration especially integrating industry demands of ICT skills in the core curriculum of the CSE program. Department is committed to encourage students/researchers to carry out innovative research in the field of Computer Science & Engineering, keeping excellence in focus and deliver quality services to match the needs of the technical education system, industry and society. CSE Department undergraduate program B.Sc.in Computer Science & Engineering, offers the students a strong background in the core technical areas and exposure to the cutting-edge technologies where their engineering contribution will be utilized. The department provides an ideal environment for the student to specialize in contemporary fields of Computer Science including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, computer Network, computer architecture, database management system, digital system design and software engineering.
